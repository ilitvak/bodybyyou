Lunch

Protein Options Chicken, Steak. Tuna, Salmon, Turkey Burgers, Ground Beef (90/10, 95/5), Ground Turkey (90/10, 95/5) 6-8 oz of either protein per meal is excellent.
Carbohydrate Options Quinoa, Brown Rice, Whole Grain Cous-Cous, Buckwheat, Yams, Whole Wheat Pasta 6-8oz of either carbohydrate is great. Carbs are stored energy so getting the healhty kind as show above is essential!
Veggie Options Peppers, Broccoli, Edamame, Caulifower, Tomatoes, Avacados, Cucumbers, Onions, Scallions, Celery, Mushrooms, Asparagus 6-8 oz of veggies is a good portion. You can never eat too many vegetables!

Lunch Options

Option 1 Chicken w/ Quinoa and Veggies 5-7 oz of chicken, 5-7 oz of Quinoa, 5-7 oz of assorted veggies.
Option 2 Steak w/ Yams and Veggies 5-7 oz of steak, 5-7 oz of Yams, 5-7 oz of veggies
Option 3 Tuna w/ cous-cous, and veggies 1 can of tuna, 5-7 oz of cous-cous, mix in onions, peppers, celery for crunch and low-fat mayo
Option 4 Salmon w/ brown rice and asparagus 5-7 oz of salmon (baked), 5-7 oz of brown rice (Not Minute-rice brand), and 5-7 oz of asparagus
Option 5 Turkey Burger Sandwich Lean Turkey Burger w/ whole wheat bread, lettuce, tomatoe, avacado, low fat cheese(optional)
Option 6 Ground Beef / Ground Turkey w/ any of the above carb choices and veggies 7-10oz of ground beef / ground turkey, 5-7 oz of choice of carbs and 5-7 oz of veggies.

At Work / Lunch Alternatives

Alternative 1 Healthy Wrap Ingredients: Whole wheat wrap, chicken/steak/salmon/tuna with lettuce, tomato, avacado, eggs whites, and onions
Alternative 2 Salad chicken/steak/salmon/tuna, mixed greens, add assortment of vegetables and take it easy on the dressing. It's a salad not a soup. Oil Recommendations for salads.
Alternative 3 Chipotle Recommendation:Burrto bowl, brown rice, chicken/steak, peppers and onions, half pinto/half black beans, and guac. There is no need to get additional fat and extra calories with sour cream, cheese and tortillas. Times per week: 1x
Alternative 4 Sandwiches Whole wheat / mutli-grain, a protein(listed above), veggies, low-fat sauce if any. Try and skip out on getting cheese.
