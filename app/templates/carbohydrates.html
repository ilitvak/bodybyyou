Carbohydrates

The misunderstood child of the family.

Healthy Carbohydrates

Complex carbohydrates are also known as starches and are made of three or more linked sugars. Grains such as bread, pasta, oatmeal and rice are complex carbs, as well as some vegetables like broccoli, corn legumes such as kidney beans and chick peas. They take the longest to digest.

Complex Carbohydrates

Grains Quinox, Oatmeal, Bread, Pasta, Rice, Buckwheat Recommendation: Opt for the whole-wheat/whole-grain options for these grains! More often than not, they will have less calories and less fat.
Nuts/Seeds and Legumes Lentils, Kidney Beans, Chick Peas, Split Peas, Soy Beans, Pinto Beans, Recommendation: Use portion size as described on back of packaging. This will avoid unnecessary calorie intake. Also by consuming the portion size you will avoid feeling bloated.
Fruits Strawberries, Apples, Pears, Grapefruit, Prunes, Tomatoes Recommendation: Have more of these options in the morning than in the evening to avoid stacking too much sugar in your diet.
Vegetables Potatoes, Onions, Okra, Dill Pickels, Carrots, Yams, Radishes, Beans, Broccoli, Spinach, Green Beans, Zucchini, Cucumbers, Asparsagus Recomendation: Depeneding on your goals, Yams > Potatoes (Unless you are trying to gain mass then It's recommended to have white potatoes), You can never go wrong with having too much vegetables, so get to it! Warning: DO NOT drench your salads in oils!

Unhealthy Carbohydrates

Simple carbohydrates are broken down quickly by the body to be used as energy. Simple carbohydrates are found naturally in foods such as fruits, milk, and milk products. They are also found in processed and refined sugars such as candy, table sugar, syrups, and soft drinks.

Simple Carbohydrates

Fruits Banana, Blackberry, Black Current, Blueberry, Cherries, Cranberries, Grapefruit, Kiwi, Melon, Orange, Peach, Plum, Raspberry Recommendation: Depending on goals, banana's are excellent pre-workout(45-1hr) min before training meals. Blueberries are also great snacks without the added worry of eating too much.
Candy Snickers, M&M's, Hershey's, Twizzlers, Milky-Way, 3-Muskateers Filled with nothing but high sugar, carbs, and fat. Recommendation: stay away!
Sodas Coke-Cola, Sprite, Fanta, Pepsi, Dr.Pepper, Mountain Dew, Root-Beer Recommendation: If you choose to indulge in drinking soda, remember that the calories and sugar add up quick. If you can switch to water and save yourself the stress of doing extra cardio than is needed.
